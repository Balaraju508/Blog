Web developer

Web development is the process of creating, designing, and maintaining websites. It involves a combination of different skills, such as programming, design, and content creation. A web developer's primary responsibility is to bring a website to life by building its functionality and ensuring it's visually appealing. A well-crafted website has the potential to attract and retain customers, provide a seamless user experience, and ultimately drive business growth.
